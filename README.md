# 🌱 AgriX – AI-Powered Smart Farming Intelligence Platform

> Empowering Farmers with AI, Weather Intelligence, Crop Diagnosis, and Multilingual Smart Assistance.


---

## 📖 Overview

AgriX is an AI-powered smart agriculture platform designed to help farmers make informed decisions through intelligent crop analysis, weather insights, multilingual assistance, and digital advisory services.

The platform combines AI-driven recommendations, real-time weather monitoring, crop health diagnostics, voice interaction, and professional reporting into a single intuitive dashboard.

AgriX aims to improve agricultural productivity while reducing water usage, crop losses, and decision-making complexity.
